## **‚û°Ô∏è How to Apply: Your Next Steps**

1.  **Check Eligibility:** Review the eligibility criteria for your desired course.
2.  **Appear for Entrance Exam:** You must have a valid score from the required entrance exam (e.g., JEE Main, CUET, CAT, MAT).
3.  **Register for Counselling:** The primary admission route is through the **UPTAC (Uttar Pradesh Technical Admission Counselling)**. You must register on the official UPTAC portal.
4.  **Choice Locking:** Select MIET Meerut (College Code: 078) as your preferred choice during the counselling process.
5.  **Seat Allotment:** Based on your rank and choices, you will be allotted a seat.
6.  **Confirm Admission:** Report to the college with your documents and admission fee to confirm your seat.
